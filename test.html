The Great Peanut Butter Panic

It was a Tuesday. Sir Reginald, a hamster of distinguished taste, discovered his personal stash of peanut butter was alarmingly low.

Important

A hamster's peanut butter supply is critical for global stability. This cannot be overstated.

He immediately assembled his team.

This text is wrapping around the team meeting. As you can see, they are very serious. Sir Reginald is on the right, looking concerned.

The Plan

The plan was simple: infiltrate the human's kitchen and secure more peanut butter.

Tip

The best time to raid the kitchen is during the human's "work from home" video calls. They are distracted by talking to a glowing rectangle.

The mission parameters were as follows:

Agent Codename Task
Bartholomew The Shadow Distraction
Penelope Tiny Paws Acquisition
Sir Reginald The Brains Supervision

The Heist

The operation was a success. Bartholomew created a diversion by running on his wheel at an unprecedented speed, generating a miniature sonic boom.

Warning

Do not attempt to break the sound barrier in a standard hamster wheel. It requires special equipment.

Penelope, using her expert climbing skills, secured the jar. The code for this maneuver is highly classified:

function securePeanutButter(agent) {
  if (agent.name === "Penelope") {
    return "SUCCESS";
  } else {
    return "FAILURE";
  }
}

Caution

The peanut butter jar is often guarded by a lid. Lid removal is a complex, two-hamster operation.

With the mission accomplished, Sir Reginald retired to his study to enjoy the spoils.
